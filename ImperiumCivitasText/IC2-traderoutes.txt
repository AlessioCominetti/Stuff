Genoa is the internationla trading hub for the Roman Empire, in particular,
for her maritime trading network. You can find all sorts of goods here.

Alexandria, founded by its namesake conqueror, it's one of the biggest
cities of the Empire. You can find all sorts of goods here.
You can sell them precious goods for quite a profit. In particular,
they provide great trades to Antiochia, to which they sell most
of their products with a discount. If you are not Antiochia,
you can buy stone, marble, flour, meat and linen from Alexandria.
You can sell to them gold, wine, iron and olive oil.
