************
*SHIPERIANS*
************
-Start with 2 citizens and 1 scout cavalry
-Start with 300 F 250 W 150 G
-Can collect favour by procuring resources, praying at temples and by
researching new technologies (20 res -> 1 favour)
-Shiperian units can be empowered with favour, turning
them into heroes or myth units
-Can upgrade buildings many times with resources and favour
-Warships move 5% faster and have +5% HP
-Researching a particular deity will make the mages
of that type cost -10%, be trained 33% faster and have their
skill recharge 50% faster

BUILDINGS
I-House, Dock, Wooden Palisade, Temple, Mill, Storehouse
II-Farm, Blacksmith, Town Center, Market, Tower, Barrack, Stable, Stone Palisade
III-Castle, Siege Workshop, Mage accademy

II-

-All land units +10% slash armor
-Buildings provide a chilling aura, that increases with favour

WATER---UJE
-All ships move 10% faster
-Docks work 1% faster for each 20 favour stored, max +50%
-All units +10% pierce armor
Tech: blessed hull: ships regen 30 hp/min
WIND---ERA
-All land units move 10% faster
-Military buildings work 25% faster(except docks)
-Dying units return 25% of their favour cost
Tech: Wind mages deal pass through damage
III-
THUNDER---BUBULIMA
-Mages cost -20% favour, they recharge their skills 33% faster
-Unlock thunder tower
-Technologies are researched 50% faster
Tech: Thunder tower +20% range
WOOD---DRURI
-Trees last 50% longer and regen themselves
-Builder and repairer work 50% faster
Tech: priest heal 100% faster
SOUND---TINGULL
-Magic Accademy produces a small tickle of faith
-Increase anti myth multiplier by 25%
Tech: Mages deal 20% true damage
IV-
DARK---ERRET
-Dark mages can apply mind control
-Mages and citizen +20% HP
Tech: Mages are trained 50% slower, but produce favour in battle
TOXIC---OXIARTE
-Walls are outfitted with thorns
-Infantry and Cavalry melee attacks inflict poison
Tech: Increase poison damage by 100%
METAL---TALIKA
-Blacksmith techs provide +50% benefits
-Myth units +10% atk for each 200 favour stores, max +50%
Tech: Buildings take -30% damage from myth units
