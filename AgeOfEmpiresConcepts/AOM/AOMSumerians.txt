***********
*SUMERIANS*
***********
Strong economy, ancient magic, slow and
durable myth units, elemental forces,
warrior priests

-Sumer build temples, who can be dedicated
to a specific god, to receive different bonuses
-Can summon myth units and heroes with an increase
in resource cost, instead of using favour

Major gods: 
Enlil: god of wind and storms
-Myth units move 15% faster
-Military techs cost -20%
-Storm: spell
-Anzu: eagle-lion, myth unit, ranged wind attack
Enki: god of water, wisdom and magic
-Farmer work 10% faster
-Spells recharge 33% faster and cost -25%
-Buildings repair themselves
-Abzu: water spirit, healer, splash damage
Inanna: god of war, love and Chaos
-infantry move 15% faster
-military land units, except siege, attack 1% faster for each
2% hp lost, cap at +30%
-Priests can convert enemies
-Lamastu: female demon, scare enemies, debuff enemy attack

Minor gods:
Nergal: Death
Utu: Sun and Justice
Ninhursag: Earth
