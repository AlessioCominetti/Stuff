*FLORANS*
*********
-Start with 4 freemen, 1 mace scout
-Start with 50 F 50 W 50 G
-Floran buildings and units are free, but takes a lot of time to build
-Can speed up build time with resources
-Freeman work 35% slower than villager, but occupy no population and cost no resources
-Favor is gained by losing units and buildings. 
For every 25 resources lost -> +1 favor
-floran units recover their full hp over 5 minutes, when not in battle or working.
-Gods techs are free
-Can turn freemen into militia, heroes or myth units
-Can turn some military soldiers into heroes or myth units
