*********
*DRAGONS*
*********
-Start with 1 dragon hunter and 1 kobold
-Start with 300 F 300 G
-Doesn't gather wood, nor can they use it, buildings cost only G
-Collect favour by losing dragon units (any ways) (100 resources -> 10 favour)
-Only have 1 god, but it can choose between different techs
-Doesn't need houses, as population is already capped
-Dragon Den units count as myth units

BUILDINGS
I-Dock, Temple
II-Animal Pen, Blacksmith, Town Center, Dragon Den, Stone wall, Tower
III-Fortress

TOWN CENTER
---Dragon Hunter---
HP 200
Atk 20 Rof 1
Range 18
Armor 30/30%
Cost 200 F 4 pop 60 sec max 20
Can only hunt, but hunts 4 times as faster
as a normal villager. Can build eco buildings:
Dock, Temple, Animal Pen, Town Center
---Kobold---
HP 100
Atk 10 Rof 1
Armor 30/30%
Speed 4
Cost 100 F 2 pop 30 sec max 10
Can only gather G, but work 3 times as fast as normal villager.
Can build Military buildings: Blacksmith, Dragon Den, Stone wall, Tower, Fortress
