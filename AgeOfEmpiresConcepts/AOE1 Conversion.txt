-No military Accademy. Hoplites are trained at the Barracks.
-Blacksmith research unit upgrades
-Market can trade and produce trade units
-can build and upgrade walls and gate
-Governement Center can be used to develop many more upgrades
-Granary develop farm upgrades
-Storage pit develop wood, stone and gold upgrades
-Governement Center develop towers and walls upgrades
-Added new units
-Added more civilizations (+14)
-Added unique units and 4 unique techs for each civilization
