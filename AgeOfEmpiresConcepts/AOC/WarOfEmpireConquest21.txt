BUILDINGS
I-40-House: grant +10 population space
I-50-Storehouse: drop site for resources
I-50-Tower: defensive structure Upgrade lvl 2 +100 G Upgrade lvl 3 +100 G
I-100-Blacksmith: research unit upgrades
III-4000 G 4000 F Wonder: used for Wonder Victory
III-200-Town Center: train villager and eco upgrades

I-10-Wall: defensive barrier
I-150-Barrack: train foot units [swordman, spearman, archer, axeman]
II-150-Stable: train mounted units [light cavalry, heavy cavalry, horse archer, camel rider]
II-150-Siege Workshop: train siege units [battering ram, artillery, catapult, ballista]
II-200-Temple: train priests
II-600-Castle: train unique units


TECHS
Melee attack upgrade I-III: melee units +2 atk: 200 F / 300 G / 100 F 300 G 60 sec
armor upgrade I-III: military units +2/2 armor: 600 F / 900 G / 300 F 900 G 180 sec
Ranged attack upgrade I-III: ranged units +2 atk: 200 F / 300 G / 100 F 300 G 60 sec
Wall upgrade I-II: improve wall hp: 500 F 700 G 120 sec / 700 F 900 G 180 sec
Logistics: reduce population cost for some units: 400 F 30 sec
Wheel barrow/Hand Cart: villager work 20% faster: 150 G 30 sec / 350 G 60 sec
Age up Castle/Imperial: 1000 F 90 sec / 1500 F 120 sec
Passion: monks +100%HP, +50% healing power, +25% speed: 200 G 60 sec
Faith: monks +33% range, all units +50% resistence*: 500 F 500 G 60 sec
Theocracy: only one monk needs to rest after conversion: 600 F 800 G 60 sec 

*all units have 134 resistence, buildings, siege, light cavalry and shock infantry have 3
times as much. Monks deplete 25 resistence per second.
