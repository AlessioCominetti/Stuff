COMMON TRAITS OF THE FLORANS
-There's no happyness. Settlers come
at a steady peace based on number of actual people.
-Florans regen health while not in battle (15 Hp/min)
-Florans settlers consume no F, and produce W (2.0-2.4) instead of F
-Florans become trees during winter: cannot move or attack, 
but cannot be harmed. Return to normal once spring arrives
-Have greater winter penalty (-1.2 F per unit, -50% atk penalty)
-Can build the House of Darts, which replace all barracks and the scout camp
-Have fewer but more powerful techs, sages produce less lore
-Can build Storehouses, which double as a mining camp and
as a woodcutter's lodge
-Market Plaza combines the trading post and the marketplace
-Temple replaces the Healer's Hut, cost Stone and
can be used to train shamans. They fight, heal and produce lore
-Can upgrade Dock to Shipyards. Sailors produce F instead of G
-Altar of Kings is an upgrade of the Castle
-Can build the Ortus. It can be assigned with villagers
to produce F. Production varies based on zone
-Silos can be placed only if there's an Ortus already built
-Doesn't utilize iron. Can mine obsidian from stone deposits.
For each 5 stone, you get 1 obsidian.
-Cannot assign settlers at runic stones. Can gather lore
by building an Ortus building nearby
-Population doesn't generate lore
