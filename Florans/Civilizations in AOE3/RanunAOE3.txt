RANUN
Aggresion, guerriglia, adaptability
-Units gain XP when nearby allied buildings
-Units can evolve with XP
-Every three military units put together will generate
a small illusion, (HP=total Hp of all three units/5, no atk)
the illusion disappearonly when 20 tiles away from the group
-Buildings change shape,size and functionality based on time,
weather and environment they are placed.
-Every time a Ranun unit dies, there's a 10% chance to spawn
an illusion(HP 20%, no atk) which last for 5 sec

Techs:
Alkaloid infusion: melee units inflict poison
Radial Bloom: buildings heal allies nearby
Symbiotic Growth: units gain XP when nearby allied buildings
Toxic Rebirth: defeated allies release a toxic cloud
Alcaloids: Poison inflict +2 dmg/sec and lowers enemy's armor by 10%
Lake's Mirror: illusions last 10 sec more
Evolved Trance: Papaverum and Circastera gain +10% Atk and HP
Tricolpato: groups of at least 3 soldiers gain +10% speed and +5% atk
Trochus's Bark: Trochodendro +20% armor
