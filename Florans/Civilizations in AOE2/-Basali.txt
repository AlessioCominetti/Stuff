The Basal florans live a simple life. Usually they find 
inspiration and guidance through nature, and often uses
their monks to find the secrets that reside in the
forests, lakes, deserts, plains and all kinds of environment.
As such, they have developed a strong connection with nature.
Basal florans can all work as villagers, albeit 50% as effective.
Since floran buildings and units are created automatically
and for free, floran villagers work less over time.
Since they live a simple life, they have fewer buildings
and technologies available them. As such, they lack
all economy technologies. Since they never developed
any kind of metal working, they don't have blacksmiths, but
instead they have developed a strong religion. Many florans
learn anything they can through their shamans, who also
act as judges and teachers. As such, the Basal Lounge
act as both a University and a Monastery. Their navy
is lackluster, but can research upgrades for free.

-Floran units and buildings regen when not in battle
-Barrack can changes functionality according to terrain
-Cannot build markets, blacksmiths, mills, lumber/mining camps, stables, gunpowder units
-Villagers work -30/20/15/10% faster
-The Basal Lounge replace Monastery and University
