-The Monocotiledoni are florans who prefer to live in remote
places in Florania. They live all over the place but rarely have
ever formed a kingdom. Despite not having much contact with other tribes,
most of them offer their services as mercenaries when money runs out.
Their life style is richer than the ones of the Gymno or Basali,
but still, they aren't the richest. Their upgrades cost less,
in particular, unit upgrades cost -50% G. 
They often live close to each other. So, all buildings provide +5
population, and can be upgrade to house more.


-Floran units and buildings regen over time while not in battle
-Buildings change functionality according to the place
they are built in
-Unit upgrades cost -50% G
-All buildings provide an additional +5 population.
Buildings can be upgrade to house +10 population, cost 50 W 50 sec.
Cannot build houses, universities, stables or gunpowder units.
Petal Shrine doubles as a Monastery and a University
-Settlements can produce floran villagers at 1/4 rate

BUILDINGS

Root Hall
train villagers, drop off point for resources.
Provide a global bonus(doesn't stack) when built in the
respective environment. Increase population by 10.
-Forest: woodcutter +5% work rate
-Swamp: farmer, forager +5% work rate
-Desert: gold miner +5% work rate
-Mountain: stone miner +5% work rate
-Beach: fisherman +5% work rate
Cost 300 W 100 S 200 sec

train Lilium, Pandan
-Desert: infantry gain +1 bonus dmg vs cavalry
train Wind Blade, Cypera
