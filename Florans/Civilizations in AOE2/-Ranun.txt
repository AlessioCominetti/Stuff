-The Ranun are savage florans who love war and raiding.
Their aggresive ways have cause scorn among all other florans.
Their tactics are about "victory at all cost". They often struggle
to keep a relevant economy, so their villagers work slower.
To compensate for their abismal living condition, they often
steal, plunder and kill nearby floran villages. They became
raiders due to the fact that the Ranun lived in mountains,
which don't have a lot of food or fertile dirt. So, they
became expanding inwards, towards the plains and lush forests.
They lived in harsh environments, so they have fewer structures
and many bonuses in environments. The Ranun used tactics
considered barbaric even amongst barbarians, such as
sacrifices, cannibalism and dark magic.

-Floran units and buildings regenerate hp when out of battle
-Floran buildings change shape and functionality in the place where
they are built in
-Villager work 35% slower
-All military units gain W by fighting and killing enemy units
-Docks provide +10 population
-Units return 25% of their W and F cost upon death
-Settlements can be upgraded to Venom Convenant or Venom Shrine.
They'll still work as settlement when upgraded
-Buildings and units cna be constructed/trained automatically and for free,
but 5 times slower. Process can be speed up with resources
-Blacksmith upgrades cost -40% F


UNITS

Buxa
magic caster who applies curses which either decrease
target's attack, armor or speed. Slow movement but great armor
Bonus: +20% effect duration in forest, +0/1 armor and +1 atk in mountain

Akon Archer
Archer with low hp and poison tipped arrows
Bonus: +1 range in mountain, +15% fire rate in swamps, +4 dmg to cavalry in forest

Akon Swordman
swordman good vs infantry
Bonus: +1 atk in forest, +10% movement speed in beaches and mountains

Akon Spearman
spearman good vs cavalry
Bonus: +1/1 armor in mountain, +4 dmg vs cavalry in desert

Actea
healer who can stealth and apply poison to enemies
Bonus: +50% poison duration in swamp and forest

Talictro
Archer whose arrows apply an armor and attack debuff
Bonus: +20% debuff duration in mountains, +10% movement speed in desert

Euptelea
Magic caster who entangle enemies with vines and poison them
Bonus: +2/2 armor in forest and swamps, +10% fire rate in beach and desert

Papaverum maceman
Heavy infantry with a periodic stun attack
Bonus: +20% stun chance in swamp, +1 atk and +1/1 armor in mountains

BUILDINGS

Root Circle
Town center who provide a global boost(non stacking) to all floran villagers
-Forest: +5% wood
-Swamp: +5% forager, farmer
-Desert: +5% gold
-Mountain: +5% stone
-Beach: +5% fish
Cost 300 W 100 S 200 sec

Venom Covenant
Train Ranun units. Change bonuses and units according to place
it is built in. Always train Akon archer/swordman/spearman
-Forest: +5 HP, +1 range to ranged, Euptelea
-Swamp: +10% fire rate and movement speed, Buxa
-Desert: +20% dmg vs cavalry, +1 atk to ranged, Talictro
-Mountain: +1/0 armor, Papaverum
Cost 200 W 50 sec

Venom Shrine
Train monks and mages. Change bonuses and units according to place
it is built in. Train Actea
-Forest/Swamp: +1 range, attacks add slow effect (-15%)
-Desert/Beach: +1/1 armor, +10% movement speed
-Mountain: +20% resistence
Cost 200 W 50 sec

Venom Tower
Defensive building with poisoned attacks. Change bonuses and units according
to place it is built in.
-Forest/Mountain: +1 atk and range
-Desert/Swamp: +2/1 armor and +25% fire rate
Cost 100 S 70 W 90 sec
