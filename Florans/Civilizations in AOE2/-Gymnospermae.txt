Bonuses:
The floran of Gymno are so in touch with nature that
refuse to consume human food. So, they don't gather
from food. They don't farm, hunt, fish. They can gather
W from berry bushes. Animals who die in los of Gymno
buildings become fertilizer, who is then used to
grow trees back into shape. All Gymno units and
buildings recover their hp over time. Gymno
palisades can grow into trees over 300 seconds.
(gain +1 W every 3 seconds). Since they don't
need F, all upgrades and units cost W instead of F.
No need to build farms, mills or fishing ships.
Due to their isolationism, they didn't develop
any sort of trading, since they get most resources
for themselves, essentially, they have become
an autarchy. So, markets and trading units are
unavailable for this civ. They also don't need
houses, since they were nomadic people and
they often clump up together to generate enough
heat to survive the winter. Moreover, florans
aren't so needy for houses in generals as they
are seen as an emblem or fortune. So, they start
with no houses but with -150 W.

-All units and buildings recover hp when not in battle. 12 hp/min for units, 150 hp/min for buildings
-Doesn't need houses, start with -200 F
-Doesn't gather F at all. Gather W from berries. All units and upgrades cost W instead of F.
-Cannot build farms, mills, fishing ships, trade units, markets, stables, gunpowder units
-buildings and units are created automatically for free, but creation time can be speed up with resources.
-Palisades grow stronger overtime, eventually become trees
-Settlement replace lumber and mining camps
