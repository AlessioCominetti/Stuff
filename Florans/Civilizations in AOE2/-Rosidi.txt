-the rosa became one of the most noble and elegant floran tribes.
They became a kingdom after the unification of the civil wars,
the rosa kingdom may be small, but their warriors are the
most decisive and strong among floran. They are often
prideful and arrogant, but they never retreat from battle.
Like the Aster, they prefer to find glory in death in battle.
Cowardice is punished and great awards are bestowed upon
the survivors of skirmishes and battles. Unlike the
Aster, only the nobles prefer to fight. The commoners
work a farmers, builders and artisans.
They have a caste system. Only the ones who belong to the
Rosa family are allowed to pursue the maximum rank
of Rosa soldiers. The land of Rosa are filled with fine sand
grains and the fishes have a lot of stone inside their bellies.

-Floran units and buildings regen hp outside battle
-Floran units and buildings are created for free automatically
but 5 times slower. Can be sped up with resources
-Military units gain +10% hp and atk 5 minutes after they spawn
-All military units get a special ability: blooming.
When in battle, +20% atk and movement speed for 10 seconds, CD 2 min
-Villagers work 30% slower but resources are deposited remotely
-farmers and fishermen generate a small tickle of S
-Villagers and buildings have thorned armor, 20% return of damage
