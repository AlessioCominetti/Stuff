Uses wisps to collect resources, hence, they don't use mines or storehouses.
Quality over quantity. Great defenses, but units take a long time to be trained.
There's a cap over wisps and elves. Legends says that an elven ranger is
as strong as 10 men. Wisps cannot gather resources, but they
are used to construct buildings. Wisps can be tasked to be
garrisoned inside specific buildings to do certain functions.
Garrison to Blacksmiths to research blacksmith upgrades.
Garrison to Universities to research upgrades.
Garrison to Shipyard/Barracks/Fortress to produce units.
Garrison to Markets to produce G, so that they might trade with other players.
The more the Wisps, the better the garrison
Upgrades and units only take times, as they don't use
resources, all upgrades are free. Wisps are limited by
the number of Temples. Houses create basic Elf units,Wisps and increase population.
Wisps count as 10 villagers. Lower Population cap.
Elves don't consume resources for upkeep.
