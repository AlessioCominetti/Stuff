ARKANDIAN EXPLORER
Character skills
Combat
Weapon mastery: increase hit chance with melee weapons
and avoid chance from being hit with said weapons
Magic Mastery: similar, but with magic
Archery: similar, but with bows
Anatomy: hit and dodge bonus which affect all weapons
Non Combat
Alchemy: increase success chance while preparing potions
Blacksmithing: similar, with weapons
Tailoring: similar, with clothes, armors
Coocking: similar, with meals
Leadership: improve hit and avoid to your party
Bargaining: increase sell price and reduce buy price at stores

Weapon caps
Critical chance(50) increase chance to land a blow which
deals twice the damage
Hit/Avoid chance (120) increase hit/Avoid chance
HP/MP drain (60) increase HP/MP drain based on percentage
Damage(100) increase damage on percentage
Pierce/Armor(60/15) ignore % of armor, or add a flat armor bonus
HP/MP up (250) add HP/MP to character
HP/MP burn (50) attack have a % chance to inflict HP/MP burn
Weaken (60) attack have a % chance to reduce foes attack power by 20%
Stun (30) attack have a % chance to make the foe skip his next turn

Trinkets
Gauntet of Fame: double fame gained in heroic deeds
Thieves gloves: after a battle, increase G gained by 150%
Ametist amulet: have a 50% to ignore all enemy spells

Spells
Bless 30 MP: +30% hit/avoid
Barrier 30 MP: +20% armor/resistence
Corrupt 40 MP: -20% armor/resistence/dodge
Heal 30 MP: heal for 50 HP
Ligthing 30 MP: deal 50 magic damage
Enchant 30 MP: next hit will deal +50% damage
Drain 30 MP: syphon 30 HP from foe
Sneak 40 MP: move past a foe
